Contribute to the STRUDEL project

You can contribute to the STRUDEL project in the following ways:

  • Share your feedback on the STRUDEL typology, design system, design UI library and our overall efforts on democratizing User eXperience resources.
  • We are planning on conducting workshops and surveys to understand your perspectives on STRUDEL. Your contributions will help us better serve the community.
  • Help us start a US-RSE UX working group. This group will share knowledge, experiences, and work towards greater recognition of UX in Science among our community.
